There are three ways to earn on Rowbound, all settled through the same escrow engine.

## 1. List in the catalog

Upload a dataset with a title, **price per record**, unit, and **provenance** (where it came from). It's validated, sampled, and screened for PII at ingest. Buyers preview the sample, then **buy any portion** — you're paid per record, and stock decrements automatically.

## 2. Fill funded requests

Browse open requests and submit data against them. Your submission is validated and **deduplicated against everything already accepted** — you're paid only for the genuinely new records you contribute, capped at the buyer's remaining demand.

## 3. Collect fresh data

Accept a funded **collection** and have your own field team submit structured entries (with geo-stamp, photo evidence, and consent where required). On finalize, the valid records settle like any other fill.

## Getting paid

- Connect a Stripe account to receive payouts.
- Pricing is **per record** — partial and over-supply both resolve cleanly against the buyer's cap.
- A platform commission applies to settled amounts (lower on plain catalog, higher on commissioned/cross-mode).

> Provenance and license aren't paperwork — they're a selling point. Buyers pay a premium for data they can prove.
